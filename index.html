Dragonfly

I took a road trip to take a girl from a camp in Texas to her home in Iowa. Her parents (friends) threw out the idea, and I took them up on it. I was so glad that I did. I got to hang out with a fascinating girl who fills her free time not with phone time and social networks but with pen, inks, pencils, etc.

She drew when she was at my house with her feet petting the dogs. She drew in the car as we drove up and chatted. For her, the phone is a way for her to get images to draw. I was fascinated by her skill. She drew this dragonfly in two days while I was at her house in Iowa. I asked her permission to use it for my return to 100 Days of Code.
